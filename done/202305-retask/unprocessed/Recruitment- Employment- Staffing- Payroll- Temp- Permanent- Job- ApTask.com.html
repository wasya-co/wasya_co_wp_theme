Optimized solutions meeting all your business needs

ApTask helps your company derive success through fast and effective recruitment at every stage of growth, from early and mid stage startups to enterprise and Fortune 500 organizations.

What we do

Permanent Staffing

Our permanent staffing solutions give you access to fully screened and qualified candidates to pick out just the right fit for your companys culture and business needs.

Temp-to-Hire

Our Temp-to-Hire solutions provide your company flexibility of decision making between contracting and full time hires. Try out the candidate before extending the offer.

Temporary Staffing

Temporary staffing solutions offer you the ability to build your staff strength without absorbing them full time. Its a great way to assist overloaded employees during critical times and keep projects moving.

Payroll Services

Use our payroll services for error free payroll management activities and to bring about new business activities that can significantly improve your productivity. ApTask provides Payrolling services at a cost that is lower than the market rate

Offshore Staffing

Offshore staffing can provide resources in any time zone that the client prefers the work to be completed in. We use a model of competency so that the specific team assigned to each project matches and complements the clients methodologies, processes, tools, and technology.

RPO

In this service bracket our in-bound team takes responsibility of all HR operations of our client such as resume management, screening candidates, interview scheduling, on-boarding facilities and more.
